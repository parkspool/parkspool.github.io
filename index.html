Welcome!

A cardano stake pool dedicated to the preservation of national and state parks across the US and the world. We are here to further promote decentralization of the cardano network, donate what we can to our community, and provide consistent returns for our delegators. We would sincerely appreciate your support at this early stage of our operation. We plan to be around for years and years to come, so please, consider being a part of a brand new community determined to make a difference.
